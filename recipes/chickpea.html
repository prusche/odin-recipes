Chickpea Recipe

a simple roasted chickpea dish with cherry tomatoes and red onions.

Ingredients

  • chickpeas, 2 cans, washed and rinsed
  • cherry tomatoes, pint
  • red onion
  • lime, sliced
  • parsley, 1/2 cup
  • garlic, 2-4 cloves, sliced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/2-1 cup water

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a roasting pan.
  2. Roast in oven at 420 for 15-20 minutes
  3. add fresh parsley, squeeze of lime, tahini dressing, sumac
