Sesame Noodle Recipe

a simple noodle dish with optional meat and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • carrots
  • onion
  • green or red pepper
  • chicken, diced
  • garlic, 2-4 cloves, minced
  • ginger, 1 tbsp

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar

Directions

  1. Prepare sauce and set aside
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Saute chicken until done, set aside
  4. Saute onion and other vegetables until done
  5. When noodles are done, drain and add to cooked vegetables, along with cooked chicken and sauce, mix thoroughly
